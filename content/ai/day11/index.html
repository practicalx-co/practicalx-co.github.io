The Short Version
What Day 11 Is About

Most people treat AI tool loyalty the way they treat phone brand loyalty — pick one, stick with it, defend it. But AI tools aren't phones. They have genuinely different strengths, pricing models, context windows, and use cases.

I started with ChatGPT. Moved to Claude to build agents. Added Gemini for research and Google integration. Discovered Groq when API costs made me reconsider what was financially possible.

Each switch taught me something the previous tool couldn't. None of them was the right tool. All of them were the right tool for that moment.

If you're still on one AI tool, you're not learning fast enough.
